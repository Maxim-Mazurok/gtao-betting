We're playing virtual roulette (without zero, just flipping a coin with 50/50 chance), counting how many times we get red vs black. Blue line shows the expectation of how many times red should win (50%).

Based on the expected number of red wins, and the actual number of red wins, we're calculating the Chi Square value. Higher value shows deviation from the expected value.

Based on the Chi Square value, we're calculating the significance level. The higher the value (closer to 1), the more chances that the deviation is due to random chance. Low values (closer to 0) mean that the game is rigged.
